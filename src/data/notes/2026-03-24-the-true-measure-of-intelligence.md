---
title: The true measure of intelligence
date: 2026-03-24
---

The true measure of intelligence is getting what you want out of life. That's it. Only happy, healthy, energetic, loved, loving, creative, helpful people are intelligent. The rest might be highly skilled, highly competent, and mostly very lucky — but without achieving those human universal goals, they didn't make "intelligent" decisions in life, and therefore, failed the game of life.

What you have to show for yourself, and what people will say at your funeral, determine a life well or not lived — and therefore, if you were "intelligent."
