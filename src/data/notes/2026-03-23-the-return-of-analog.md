---
title: The return of analog — "retro bros" are the next hipsters
date: 2026-03-23
---

People will soon come back to "retro." We got hipsters, but soon we'll get retro people — going back to old technologies, "analog," "slow consumption."

Vinyls, cassettes, old film projectors for their consumption. Analog cameras already exist because it's a creative process. But now analog stuff is going to be trendy for consumption too, because: Spotify has been making you like slop music for years and is now actively pushing it through your ears. Netflix is using AI slop in all the processes of their productions. Fast, easy, abundant has become slop — unrelatable, hollow.

People will want to go back to slow, hard, rich, and imperfect. I'm also betting on screen printing, collage, and creative processes that are complicated, manual, and imperfect. Manual writing, actual letter correspondence, gifting drawings, paintings, handwritten poems, etc.
